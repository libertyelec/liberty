Liberty Integrated Solutions, Inc. is a fully licensed, contracting company serving the Philadelphia region.


Liberty brings integrity, reliability, experience, efficiency and safety to every project we do. We are a dynamic, performance driven contracting company with an unmatched reputation for professionalism. Our history is built on a wealth of electrical experience and in-depth knowledge in all facets of electrical, data and low voltage construction.

Our dedication to collaboration and team building sets us apart from our competition - we strive for excellance every day, on every job.

Liberty's Philosophy is based on three pillars of customer service Quality, Communication and Value. By adhering to these three basic standards our clients can be assured that they have the personal commitment of every member of our team with the leadership to provide the best and highest quality level of customer service available.
